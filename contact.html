Frequently asked questions

How accurate are the AI translations?

Our AI strives for high accuracy, but translations may contain errors, especially with complex dishes, regional specialties, or allergen information. We strongly recommend verifying important information, especially allergens and dietary restrictions, directly with restaurant staff.

How do I request a refund?

Email billing@whatthemenu.com within 24 hours of purchase with your transaction details. Refunds are processed within 5 business days to your original payment method. Processing fees are non-refundable.

Do I need to create an account?

No, account creation is optional. You can use our core translation features without an account. Creating an account provides additional features like translation history and personalized settings.

How is my image data protected?

Your images are encrypted during transmission and processing. We automatically delete images within 30 days. Images are processed using OpenAI's secure services and are not used to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

What if the app isn't working properly?

First, try refreshing the page or restarting the app. If issues persist, contact support@whatthemenu.com with details about the problem, including your device type, browser, and any error messages you're seeing.

Can I use this app for commercial purposes?

Our standard service is for personal use only. For commercial licensing, API access, or business integrations, please contact partnerships@whatthemenu.com to discuss custom solutions.
