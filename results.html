🥗 Appetizers
Bruschetta Classica
Toasted bread with fresh tomatoes, basil, and garlic
▼
Traditional Italian appetizer with crispy bread, fresh tomatoes, basil and garlic. Light, refreshing starter perfect for sharing. Usually served as antipasto and pairs well with wine. The bread is typically rubbed with garlic for extra flavor depth.
Calamari Fritti
Golden fried squid rings with marinara sauce
▼
Crispy battered and fried squid rings served with tangy marinara dipping sauce. Popular Mediterranean seafood appetizer with tender interior and crunchy exterior. Great for sharing and pairs well with white wine or beer.

🍝 Main Courses
Spicy Arrabbiata
Penne pasta in spicy tomato sauce
▼
⚠️ Spicy pasta with penne in fiery tomato sauce containing chili peppers. Traditional Roman "angry" recipe that packs serious heat. May be too intense for sensitive palates. The dish gets its name from the spiciness level.
Risotto ai Funghi
Creamy rice with wild mushrooms and parmesan
▼
Rich, creamy Italian rice dish slowly cooked with wild mushrooms and finished with parmesan cheese. Comfort food that's hearty and satisfying. The rice has a creamy texture while maintaining a slight bite. Perfect for vegetarians.
🍰 Desserts
Tiramisu
Coffee-soaked ladyfingers with mascarpone cream
▼
Classic Italian dessert featuring layers of coffee-soaked ladyfinger cookies and rich mascarpone cream, dusted with cocoa powder. Creamy, indulgent finish to any meal. The name means "pick me up" in Italian, referring to the coffee content.
